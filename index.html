Quick Insight

Hello! My name is Hayley Allard-Raucci. I am currently a senior at the University of Connecticut, majoring in Computer Science & Engineering and minoring in Mathematics. I spend my free time either swimming, running, or playing Sims.

Life as a Senior

For starters, I am only taking two classes to finish off my requirements for graduation this May. I am enrolled in Digital Systems Design and Senior Design. Digital Systems Design is a class focused on hardware programming in VHDL. I chose to take this class not only to enhance my hardware skills but also for my interest in circuits. Senior Design consists of a project sponsored by either UConn or an industry. I am working with three others on Energy Load Forecasting sponsored by POWWR. The goal of this project is to use machine learning to produce a model that will predict energy consumption strictly using recorded hourly usage and weather data from the past. We successfully created a model with a mean absolute percentage error of 3.63%, an improvement from our goal of 10% error. This semester we will move on in creating an additional model using data in larger areas. This will force us to group weather station data by zone to find their average temperature and hourly usage data.

When I am not at school, I am at my internship with ISO New England. I have been interning here since June 2018. I was accepted into their IT-Day Ahead Support team for the summer internship program. During this program, I gained more knowledge and experience in the electrical power systems industry. We visited a trash plant, a wind farm, and a nuclear powerplant. ISO held lunches with VPs to not only hear their experiences but, ask them questions. Once summer ended, I was asked if I would like to continue my internship through out the school year with the option to switch departments. I am now in their Operations Performance, Training and Integration team located in Windsor, CT.

If I am not too busy working on homework or away in Windsor, I am hanging out with my friends. Our favorite activities are hiking, going out to eat, and staying at home to watch a movie. The majority of my friends are also computer science majors so we are constantly discussing anything related to computers.
