Life as a Senior

For starters, I am only taking two classes to finish off my requirements for graduation this May. I am enrolled in Digital Systems Design and Senior Design. Senior Design consists of a project sponsored by either UConn or an industry. I am working with three others on Energy Load Forecasting sponsored by POWWR. The goal of this project is to use machine learning to produce a model that will predict energy consumption strictly using recorded hourly usage and weather data from the past. We successfully created a model with a mean absolute percentage error of 3.63%, an improvement from our goal 10% error. We will move on to creating an additional model using data from more weather stations.
